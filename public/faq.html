FAQ

What is Donathon?

Donathon is a service that aims to increase the amount of donations to natural disaster relief charities worldwide to improve our precious world. Donathon is also a service where users can easily see real-time disasters, and fundraise/donate them on the spot. Along the way, users can see and meet other people who are just as charitable, and users can also individually win points. Users with the most points can get gifts of appreciation/prizes from us.

How do I donate?

Click on one of the natural disaster icons on the map in index.html in order to donate some money! You will get points if you do so.

What kind of things can I win?

You get virtual badges, like how StackOverflow do their badges, when you donate or are ranked as a top donator. Not only that, but you can also win the opportunity to travel and go to the place you donated and volunteer there, or win some materials for fundraising, or win some t-shirts!
